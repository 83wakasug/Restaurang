Sunny Side Bistro is a fine dining restaurant located in the heart of the city.

Our concept revolves around offering exquisite dishes crafted from the finest local ingredients, with a focus on sustainability and freshness.
We pride ourselves on creating memorable dining experiences with every visit.

From our humble beginnings as a family-owned bistro to now serving gourmet meals to food enthusiasts worldwide, we’ve stayed true to our commitment to quality and creativity.
Our culinary philosophy celebrates the beauty of simple ingredients, expertly prepared and beautifully presented.
